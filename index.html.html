Past Experiences

When I was still a Psychology major, I landed myself a research internship for Columbia University. I was very much intimidated throughout the application process and even more so when I started training, but I put my big boy pants on and was at the internship for about a year.

After about a year of the internship, I decided to leave because I knew I no longer wanted to be in the Psych field. I kind of spent the time after that playing music and trying to find myself. One thing led to another and now I'm learning to code.
